OpenVine is a camera app inspired by the mechanics of the classic Vine application. It allows you to create short, looping videos with ease.

<b>Features:</b>

<ul>
<li>The app records only while you keep the big red button pressed.</li>
<li>You can release the button to change angles or scenes. Once you have recorded a total of 6 seconds, the app automatically stitches all the clips together into a single video file.</li>
<li>This is purely a camera utility. There is no external server, no account login, and no cloud tracking.</li>
<li>Videos are stored locally on your device. You can easily forward them to instant messengers, email, or social networks using the built-in Android share functionality.</li>
</ul>

<b>Artistic effects:</b>

The selfie camera may record upside-down on certain devices. This is definitely not a bug but an artistic choice. If you do not want to record upside down, rotate your phone by 180 degrees before recording selfies.
